Shichi Hoshi Dining

is where users can enjoy top-notch Japanese cuisine with a touch of history and sophistication. Easily book a table, find the location, and share your thoughts through reviews. Embark on a delicious journey with Shichi Hoshi for an elevated Japanese dining experience.

Peppa Rolls

is a fun dice game where two players take turns rolling a die to accumulate points. Players can choose to roll as many times as they want, but if they roll a 1, they lose all points for that turn. The first player to reach a permanent score of 100 wins, adding an element of strategy to the fun, quick-paced game.

PHINMA Diner

an app that simplifies PHINMA-University of Pangasinan's cafeteria tasks. Users can purchase stub points with real money at the cafeteria using a QR code scanner. Once done, users can now explore a variety of food and drink options along with exciting deals for a convenient dining experience
